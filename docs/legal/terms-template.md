# Terms Of Service Template

This is a starter template, not legal advice.

## Authorized Use

Users must confirm they are authorized to run searches for educational, defensive, investigative, compliance, or self-assessment purposes.

## Prohibited Use

- Harassment, stalking, doxxing, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Credential theft or account takeover.
- Circumventing access controls.
- Uploading or processing raw secrets without authorization.

## Reports

Reports are source-attributed and confidence-scored. Users must manually verify findings before relying on them.
